03
Bio-Degradable Polyester Fabric
生物降解涤纶系列面料

我们开发了一系列以再生可降解涤纶纤维为原料的牛仔面料，这种纤维以回收海洋上漂浮的塑料瓶为原料，通过熔喷纺丝方法制作成纱线。其产品生物降解速度是普通涤纶的12倍。大大减少了对环境的污染。

Blue Diamond developed a series of denim fabric made by Bio-Degradable polyester fiber. This fiber is made by collecting PET bottles floating on the ocean. The speed of degradation is 12 times faster than the conventional polyester which greatly reduced environmental pollution.

我们开发了一系列以再生可降解涤纶纤维为原料的牛仔面料，这种纤维以回收海洋上漂浮的塑料瓶为原料，通过熔喷纺丝方法制作成纱线。其产品生物降解速度是普通涤纶的12倍。大大减少了对环境的污染。

One-year statistics from the testing concluded:

• EcoSure BioBlast nonwoven had 72.5% biodegradation

• Control polyester nonwoven had 6.0% biodegradation

• Control polyethylene sample had 5.2% biodegradation

• Cellulosic (wood/paper) sample had 75.7%biodegradation

环保的染料带给消费者环保的生活
Eco-friendly Dye

我们使用液体染料，相较于粉状染料，可大幅减少化学品和水的用量，减少排放污染，使工人拥有更健康、更安全的工作环境。

Blue Diamond uses liquid dyestuff which can greatly decrease the consumption of chemicals and water, reduce the discharge of pollution, create more healthy and safer working condition.
