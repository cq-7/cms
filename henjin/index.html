关于恒进
About

邢台恒进纺织有限公司位于河北省邢台市经济开发区，是华北地区出口量最大的牛仔面料制造企业，国家高新技术企业。公司集设计、研发、生产、销售为一体，专业生产“蓝钻”品牌牛仔面料，直供国际牛仔服装 Levi’s，Lee，AEO，G-star等二十多个知名品牌，产品出口30多个国家和地区，深受客户喜爱，Blue Diamond Denim 享誉海内外。 公司拥有专业技术人员400余人，其中具有中、高级专业技术人员150余人，拥有十余项发明和实用新型专利。通过了ISO9001质量体系认证、ISO14001环境管理体系认证和OEKO-TEX Standard100生态纺织品认证。公司建有标准化实验室，并通过 Levi’s、 AEO 联合BV所做的国际标准化实验室认证。 作为BCI会员，我们率先使用良好棉花，承担社会责任。公司开发的一系列再生纤维品种，通过了GRS和RCS认证。为引领市场导向，公司坚持可持续发展战略，使用环保粘胶、可降解涤纶、再生天丝、咖啡纤维、汉麻等多种不同环保原料，研发出了吸湿排汗、凉爽保暖、抗菌、防紫外线、可降解等新型牛仔面料。我们严格管控化学品的使用,做到了有毒化学物质零排放，由BV按照 ZDHC 标准定期监测，并在其官网上发布。公司恪守“以诚取信，以质取胜”的经营理念，获得企业信用AAA级认证。公司将秉承“科技创新、绿色发展”的企业精神，奉献社会。

Blue Diamond,located in the Economic Development Zone of Xingtai,Hebei ,China is the largest exporter of denim fabric in North China and a national high-technology enterprise, specializing in R&D, production and sales. Blue Diamond is the nominated supplier of Levi's, Lee, AEO, G-star and other more than 20 well-known brands. Its products are exported to more than 30 countries and regions, which are trusted by customers. Blue Diamond Denim is well known both at home and abroad. The company has more than 400 technicians including 150 junior and senior technicians. It owns more than ten invention and utility model patents. It obtained ISO9001 quality system certification, ISO14001 environmental management system certification and OEKO-TEX Standard100 eco-textile certification. The company has a standardization laboratory and was certified by Levi’s and AEO. As a BCI member, we take the lead in using BCI cotton and taking social responsibility. The company has developed a series of recycled fabric that has passed GRS and RCS certification. In order to lead the international market, the company adheres to the sustainable development strategy, using eco-made viscose, bio-degradable polyester, refibra, coffee fiber, hemp and other various eco-friendly raw materials. Blue Diamond developed moisture wicking, cool and warm, antibacterial, anti- UV, degradable and other functional denim fabric. We strictly control the use of chemicals and achieve zero emissions of toxic chemicals. The reports are regularly monitored by BV in accordance with ZDHC standards and published on its official website. The company adheres to the business philosophy of “Sincere Cooperation, Excellent Quality” and has obtained the AAA certification of corporate credit. The company will adhere to the enterprise spirit of "Technology Innovation, Green Development" to contribute to society.
